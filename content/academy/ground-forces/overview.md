# Ground Forces Academy

Welcome to the Ground Forces Academy—your comprehensive training program for mastering tank combat in War Thunder.

This is not a quick reference guide. This is a structured military-style training program designed to transform you from a novice tanker into a skilled commander. Each chapter builds on previous concepts, creating a complete understanding of ground combat that applies at every Battle Rating.

## Who This Academy Is For

**New Players**: If you're just starting with ground forces, this academy provides the foundation you need. Start with Chapter 1 and progress sequentially.

**Intermediate Players**: If you understand basics but struggle with consistency, this academy will help you identify and fix gaps in your knowledge. Review fundamentals, then focus on advanced chapters.

**Experienced Players**: Even veterans can benefit from reviewing core principles and learning new perspectives on familiar concepts. Use this as a reference and refresher.

## What You'll Learn

This academy module consists of progressive chapters covering everything from basic tank mechanics to advanced competitive strategies. You'll learn:

- **Fundamental Mechanics**: How armor, penetration, and shell types actually work
- **Positioning Theory**: Where to be, when to be there, and why it matters
- **Vehicle Roles**: How to play each vehicle type effectively
- **Map Mastery**: Understanding terrain, lanes, and control points
- **Advanced Tactics**: Crossfires, rotations, and team coordination
- **Meta Understanding**: How the game's systems create patterns of play

## How This Academy Works

Each chapter follows a structured format:

1. **Core Concepts**: The fundamental principles you need to understand
2. **Practical Applications**: How to apply these concepts in actual matches
3. **Examples and Scenarios**: Real-world situations that illustrate the concepts
4. **Common Mistakes**: What to avoid and why
5. **Training Drills**: Exercises to practice and reinforce learning
6. **Progression Path**: What to study next

> **Pro Tip:** Don't rush through chapters. Read each one carefully, practice the drills, and apply the concepts in-game before moving to the next chapter. Mastery comes from understanding, not speed-reading.

### Course Structure

**Fundamentals (Chapters 1-7)**
- Tank fundamentals (shell types, armor, penetration)
- Armor mechanics and angles
- Weak spot identification and aiming
- Positioning and map awareness
- Line of sight and spotting mechanics
- Mobility tactics
- Teamwork and coordination

**Vehicle Type Specialization (Chapters 8-11)**
- Main Battle Tank (MBT) playstyle
- Light tank and scout tactics
- Tank Destroyer doctrine
- Self-Propelled Anti-Aircraft (SPAA) tactics

**Advanced Tactics (Chapters 12-19)**
- BR tier-specific strategies
- Urban and long-range combat
- Advanced positioning techniques
- Weak spot mastery
- Meta analysis and adaptation
- Competitive play strategies

## Learning Path

Start with Chapter 1 and progress through each chapter in order. Each chapter builds on concepts from previous chapters, so following the sequence will give you the best learning experience.

## Estimated Time

- **Full Module**: 3-4 hours of reading
- **Per Chapter**: 10-15 minutes
- **Practice Time**: Apply concepts in-game between chapters

## Prerequisites

- Basic understanding of War Thunder controls
- Completed New Player Path (recommended)
- Experience with at least 5-10 matches in ground forces

## Training Programs

To help you structure your learning, we've created focused training programs. Choose the one that matches your current skill level and goals.

### Two-Week Ground Improvement Plan

**For**: Players who understand basics but want to improve consistency and win rate.

**Week 1: Fundamentals Reinforcement**
- **Day 1-2**: Review armor mechanics and angling (Chapter 2)
- **Day 3-4**: Master weakspot identification and aiming (Chapter 3)
- **Day 5-6**: Practice positioning and map awareness (Chapters 4-5)
- **Day 7**: Review and practice all fundamentals in test drive

**Week 2: Tactical Application**
- **Day 8-9**: Learn vehicle roles and playstyles (Chapters 8-11)
- **Day 10-11**: Practice micro-tactics: peeking, shoot-and-scoot (Chapter 4)
- **Day 12-13**: Master macro-tactics: lanes, crossfires, rotations (Chapter 5)
- **Day 14**: Full review and focused practice in matches

**Daily Practice**: 30 minutes of focused practice per day, plus 2-3 matches applying that day's concepts.

### Four-Week Complete Ground Mastery Program

**For**: New players or those wanting comprehensive mastery.

**Week 1: Foundation**
- Complete Chapters 1-3 (Fundamentals, Armor, Weakspots)
- Focus on understanding, not speed
- Practice each concept in test drive before moving on

**Week 2: Positioning and Awareness**
- Complete Chapters 4-5 (Positioning, Map Awareness)
- Practice positioning drills daily
- Play matches focusing only on positioning

**Week 3: Vehicle Mastery**
- Complete Chapters 8-11 (Vehicle Roles)
- Practice each vehicle type in test drive
- Build and test lineups

**Week 4: Advanced Tactics**
- Complete remaining chapters
- Focus on team coordination and meta understanding
- Review all previous chapters

**Daily Commitment**: 1 hour of study + 1 hour of practice per day.

### Quick Reference: Ground Tactics Checklist

Use this checklist to ensure you're applying core concepts:

**Before Each Match:**
- [ ] Reviewed map layout and common routes
- [ ] Checked lineup BR spread (within 0.7 BR)
- [ ] Selected appropriate vehicles for map type
- [ ] Reviewed weakspots for common enemy vehicles

**During Match:**
- [ ] Using cover effectively
- [ ] Checking minimap every 5-10 seconds
- [ ] Angling armor when appropriate
- [ ] Aiming for weakspots, not center mass
- [ ] Moving after firing (shoot-and-scoot)
- [ ] Supporting teammates, not lone-wolfing

**After Match:**
- [ ] Reviewed what went well
- [ ] Identified mistakes and how to avoid them
- [ ] Noted positioning errors
- [ ] Planned improvements for next match
