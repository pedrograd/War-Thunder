# Fulda Gap - Complete Tactical Guide

Fulda Gap is one of War Thunder's largest and most strategically complex maps, offering varied terrain and multiple engagement areas.

## Map Overview

**Size**: Large

**Terrain Type**: Mixed (fields, forests, urban areas, hills)

**Key Features**:
- Multiple spawn points
- Varied elevation changes
- Urban areas in center
- Open fields on flanks
- Forest cover throughout

**Best For**: All vehicle types, but especially mobile vehicles and tanks with good gun depression

## Spawn Analysis

### North Spawn

**Advantages**:
- Good initial positions on hills
- Can control central areas early
- Multiple routes available
- Good cover options

**Disadvantages**:
- Longer route to southern objectives
- Can be outflanked from east/west

**Initial Routes**:
- **Central Route**: Direct to town, good for medium tanks
- **West Route**: Flanking route, good for light tanks
- **East Route**: Support route, good for slower tanks

### South Spawn

**Advantages**:
- Closer to southern objectives
- Good defensive positions
- Multiple escape routes

**Disadvantages**:
- More exposed initial positions
- Harder to control central areas

**Initial Routes**:
- **Central Route**: Direct to town, fast but exposed
- **East Route**: Support route, more protected
- **West Route**: Flanking route, risky but rewarding

## Key Positions

### Central Town

**Characteristics**: Urban area with buildings and streets

**Best Vehicles**: Medium tanks, light tanks

**Strategy**:
- Use buildings for cover
- Control key intersections
- Support from second line
- Avoid main streets when possible

**Positions**:
- Buildings overlooking main street
- Second story windows for observation
- Side streets for flanking

### Hill Positions

**Characteristics**: Elevated positions with good fields of fire

**Best Vehicles**: Tanks with good gun depression (USA, UK)

**Strategy**:
- Hull down positions
- Long-range engagement
- Control approaches to objectives
- Reposition when spotted

**Key Hills**:
- **North Hill**: Overlooks central area
- **East Hill**: Covers approach routes
- **West Hill**: Flanking positions

### Forest Areas

**Characteristics**: Tree cover provides concealment

**Best Vehicles**: Light tanks, TDs

**Strategy**:
- Use trees for concealment
- Ambush unsuspecting enemies
- Support from cover
- Avoid open areas

## Vehicle-Specific Strategies

### Main Battle Tanks (MBTs)

**Positioning**:
- Use hull down positions on hills
- Control central areas
- Support team from second line
- Avoid open fields

**Routes**:
- Follow central routes
- Use hills for protection
- Control key intersections

### Light Tanks

**Positioning**:
- Use mobility to flank
- Control forest areas
- Scout for team
- Avoid direct confrontations

**Routes**:
- Use flanking routes
- Forest paths
- Support from sides

### Tank Destroyers

**Positioning**:
- Long-range positions on hills
- Hidden in forests
- Cover key approaches
- Second line support

**Routes**:
- Support routes
- Hill positions
- Forest hideouts

### SPAA

**Positioning**:
- Behind friendly tanks
- Near objectives
- Protected positions
- Clear fields of fire

**Routes**:
- Follow main force
- Protect key areas
- Avoid front line

## Nation-Specific Strategies

### USA

**Strengths**: Gun depression, mobility

**Strategy**:
- Use hull down positions on hills
- Control elevated areas
- Support team with positioning
- Use mobility to reposition

**Recommended Vehicles**: M4 Sherman, M18 Hellcat, M24 Chaffee

### Germany

**Strengths**: Gun penetration, accuracy

**Strategy**:
- Long-range positions
- Control key intersections
- Support from second line
- Use accuracy advantage

**Recommended Vehicles**: Pz.IV, StuG III, Tiger I (if at BR)

### USSR

**Strengths**: Armor, firepower

**Strategy**:
- Brawl in urban areas
- Control central town
- Use armor advantage
- Close-range combat

**Recommended Vehicles**: T-34, KV-1, IS-1

## BR Tier Analysis

### Low Tier (1.0-4.0)

**Meta**: Fast-paced, mobility focused

**Strategy**: Use speed to control positions, light tanks excel

**Key Positions**: Central town, forest areas

### Mid Tier (4.0-7.0)

**Meta**: Tactical positioning, armor matters more

**Strategy**: Control hills and intersections, support teamwork

**Key Positions**: Hills, central town, key intersections

### High Tier (7.0-11.0)

**Meta**: Long-range engagement, positioning critical

**Strategy**: Hull down positions, control key areas, avoid exposure

**Key Positions**: All hill positions, central objectives

## Common Mistakes

1. **Exposing in open fields**: Always use cover
2. **Not using gun depression**: Missing hull down opportunities
3. **Staying in one position too long**: Reposition frequently
4. **Ignoring flanks**: Always check for flankers
5. **Not supporting team**: Play with team, not solo

## Tips for Success

1. **Control hills early**: Elevation is key advantage
2. **Use cover constantly**: Never stay in open
3. **Check flanks**: Light tanks will flank
4. **Support team**: This map rewards teamwork
5. **Have escape routes**: Always plan retreat paths
6. **Adapt to situation**: Map has many playstyles
